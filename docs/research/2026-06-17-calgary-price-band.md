# Calgary One-Time Price Band — real published prices

**Date:** 2026-06-17 · **Method:** deep-research harness, 5 angles → 17 sources → 54 claims → 25 verified (**19 confirmed, 6 killed**). Goal: pin a defensible single flat fee from *real vendor prices*, not blog "cost-guide" ranges (those kept failing verification — and several did again here).

> **Defensible single flat fee: ~$1,997 CAD** (band $1,500–$2,500) for a design-led, client-owns-everything, CMS-included local-business site. Sits clearly above the $299–$999 budget-template floor and the "cheap" perception, at/just above the closest real analog (Agency7's $1,497 CMS tier, CodeWeb's $2,450 Pro), and far below the $7,500–$15,000+ custom-agency tier.

## The number

**Recommended single flat fee: ~$1,997 CAD** (round, premium-over-template, just under the psychological $2,500).
- **Why this and not lower:** the $299–$999 floor is explicitly "cheap template" positioning; pricing there signals low quality and forfeits the design-led differentiation. $1,997 reads as quality-not-cheapest while staying accessible.
- **Why this and not higher:** the $7,500+ tier is genuinely-custom agency work (8–12 wk, custom design from scratch) — a different product than our pipeline-built, design-system site.
- **Anchor logic:** matches/just exceeds Agency7's $1,497 CMS tier and CodeWeb's $2,450 Pro — the real Alberta comparables that, like us, bundle ownership + CMS one-time. **Up to $2,495 is defensible** if we foreground design quality + local SEO + JSON-LD/conversion completeness as the explicit differentiators.

This is a **reasoned synthesis from cited primary prices**, not a figure any single vendor publishes.

## Caveats / flags
- **"One-time" and "CAD" are inferred** on most pages (structural contrast with monthly SEO/care; .ca vendors) — strong but not verbatim.
- **"From/starting at"** prices (Agency7, Pixel Army, GrowME) are floors; real totals can exceed.
- **Two cleanest analogs (Agency7, Pixel Army) are Edmonton**, not Calgary — same Alberta market. Calgary-specific primaries: SBQS, Sunstone, CodeWeb, GrowME.
- **No Fiverr/Upwork gig prices verified** — marketplace pages returned unreliable/empty; that primary data point is still missing.
- **Refuted — do NOT cite:** GrowME "$5,000–$50,000"; all OxOne figures ($497/5pg, $1,500-start, "$2,500–$5,000 most common," its tier breakdown) — OxOne claims failed verification (0-3 / 0-2 / 1-2); Chameleon's "$2,500–$8,000" is a blog estimate, not a vendor price (runs high vs reality).
- **No verified Calgary shop offers a true single flat fee** — all use tiers/ranges. Our single-fee model has **no confirmed local precedent** → a genuine differentiator.
