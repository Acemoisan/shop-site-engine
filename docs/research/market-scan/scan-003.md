# Scan 003 — AI website generators + CMS/self-edit posture (PARTIAL)

**Date:** 2026-06-16 · **Method:** deep-research harness, 5 angles → 21 sources → 100 claims → 25 reached verification. ⚠️ **Run was cut off by a session/rate limit mid-verification** — only **4 claims got full 3-vote confirmation**; the rest **abstained (0-0) because the limit blocked the voters, and the synthesis step never ran.**
**Important:** the "Unverified" items below are **NOT refuted** — they are real data points extracted from primary vendor pages by the fetch agents, just not adversarially re-verified. Treat as *probable, single-source, needs re-confirm*. A re-run is queued.

> **Headlines (provisional):** (1) The AI website generators are **all subscriptions with hosting lock-in** — opposite of our model; their threat is to the DIY-owner segment, not to done-for-you. (2) **Self-edit CMS is NOT as rare as scan-002 implied** — done-for-you builders UENI and Jottful both ship a client editor. That partially **weakens "self-edit CMS" as our moat** and pushes the wedge back toward design quality + true ownership + no recurring fee.

## CONFIRMED (full 3-vote) — AI website generators

### 10Web AI · subscription · `high`
- Recurring subscription, monthly/annual. **AI Starter $10/mo · AI Premium $15/mo · AI Ultimate $23/mo.** Annual discounts 50%/60%/65% at 12/24/36 mo.
- Source: https://10web.io/pricing-platform/

### Butternut AI · subscription · `high`
- SaaS, two paid tiers: **Starter $20/mo · Pro $69/mo** (25% off yearly). Recurring, not one-time.
- Source: https://www.butternut.ai/pricing

### Mixo · code-export gated · `high`
- Lets users download source code **only as a paid add-on** (separate payment link), not by default → soft lock-in.
- Source: https://www.mixo.io/help/can-i-download-the-source-code-for-my-mixo-site/

## UNVERIFIED (single-source, limit-blocked — re-confirm next run)

### New competitors discovered (notable)
- **UENI** — done-for-you website service, **7-day turnaround**, pro copywriting; **subscription: $79 one-time setup + $24.99/mo**. Crucially, **gives clients a self-edit CMS/editor** (pages, products, FAQs, blog). → done-for-you + self-edit coexist. Source: https://ueni.com/en-us/done-for-you-website
- **Jottful** — **$99 one-time setup + monthly sub** ($65/mo, or $55/mo yearly = $660/yr; Grow $79, Pro $169). Ships a self-edit CMS called the **"Magical Editor"** (text + images) standard on all plans. Source: https://jottful.com/pricing/

### AI generator pricing/model (provisional)
- **Wix AI** — subscription, ~**$17/mo (Lite, annual) → $159/mo (Business Elite)**. Source: tech.yahoo.com Wix-vs-Hostinger article.
- **Hostinger AI** — subscription, **$2.99/mo intro → $11.99 renew** (Basic), **$3.99 → $13.99** (Plus). Same source.
- **Durable** — AI builders are subscription; **site becomes inaccessible when the subscription expires** (30–60 day grace before deletion). Source: durable.com blog.
- **10Web** — runs on own managed/proprietary hosting (Google Cloud); hosting bundled (10GB Starter / 200GB Pro), implying lock-in. (abstained)
- **Butternut** — hosting bundled w/ SSL/backups; targets DIY owners (Starter) + agencies (Pro); custom-code embed but no full code-export terms. (abstained)
- **Framer** — known to lock users into Framer hosting (community export tools exist). Source: indiehackers/letaiworkforme (in source list, claims not surfaced).
- **Lovable / v0** — dev-grade AI builders with code export (in source list; claims not surfaced this run).
- **Code export** generally is NOT a standard feature across builders → lock-in is the norm; export is the anti-lock-in mechanism. Source: hocoos.

### Re-confirmations of prior peers (consistent with scan-001/002, just not re-voted here)
- **Simple Built Co.** — one-time $599 + $150/page, no contracts, client owns outright, host anywhere, hosting/maint optional after delivery; **done-for-you copywriting, NO self-edit CMS.**
- **Sitebolt.co** — one-time $1,500/$2,500 (50/50), client owns; **done-for-you, NO self-edit CMS** — post-launch changes only via 30/60-day support windows + build-phase revisions.
