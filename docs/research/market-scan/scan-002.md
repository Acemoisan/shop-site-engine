# Scan 002 — Calgary/Alberta one-time-fee rivals + GTM funnels

**Date:** 2026-06-16 · **Method:** deep-research harness, 5 angles → 22 sources → 107 claims → 25 verified. **24 confirmed, 1 killed.**
**Angles covered:** Calgary/Alberta flat-fee competitors; ownership/self-edit positioning; GTM benchmark of the productized shops; free-audit lead funnels; channel mix.

> **Headlines:** (1) We finally have **Calgary-anchored direct rivals** — Calgary Web Studios, CodeWeb, Hexagon Media, Clio Websites (+ Websites.ca's Calgary page). (2) The **dominant GTM funnel across one-time-fee shops is a FREE website audit/grade** that captures email + URL — i.e. *exactly our planned funnel is the table-stakes mechanic, not a unique edge.* (3) The strongest one-time players foreground **"you own everything / no lock-in / yours forever"** as their headline positioning lever — the same wedge we're counting on.

## GTM / lead-gen pattern (cross-competitor synthesis) · `high`

**The repeatable funnel for productized one-time-fee shops = the FREE website audit/grade.** Captures email + site URL, returns an assessment. Verified at:
- **Sitebolt** — "Free Site Grade": *"Drop your email and we'll send an honest grade…where it's leaking customers"* → Overall/Mobile/SEO/Speed metrics, reply <24h.
- **Agency7** — AI audit + cost calculator + free consult.
- **Fairway** — free competitor-keyword + performance comparison report.
- **Clio** — assessment tool + price estimator.

Secondary repeatable funnels: **free interactive cost calculators/estimators** and **free 15-min consults.**

**Positioning lever:** the strongest one-time players foreground **explicit ownership** — Sitebolt *"fully transferred, no strings"*, Agency7 *"you own everything… no agency lock-in"*, Simple Built Co. *"own it outright… take it anywhere."*

### ⚠️ Strategic implication (note, not synthesis)
Our two headline differentiators — the **free-audit funnel** and the **ownership pitch** — are **both already standard** among the strongest one-time competitors (Agency7, Sitebolt). They are table stakes, not moats. The open differentiators left: **self-edit CMS** (only Agency7 confirmed to ship one; Simple Built/Sitebolt are done-for-you, no CMS) and **design quality + LocalBusiness schema**.
