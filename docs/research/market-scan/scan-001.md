# Scan 001 — Re-verify abstained players + one-time-fee peers (CA + US)

**Date:** 2026-06-16 · **Method:** deep-research harness, 5 angles → 19 sources → 87 claims → 25 verified (3-vote adversarial). **25/25 confirmed, 0 killed.**
**Angles covered:** Mind2Market/Hibu/Squarespace/Designjoy/Conversion Factory re-verification; Canadian one-time-fee shops by city; US flat-fee/client-owns builders.

> **Headline:** Studio0rbit's exact model (one-time fee + client-owns-everything + self-edit CMS) has a small set of true peers. The closest structural matches found this round are **Agency7 (Edmonton)** and **Edmonton Web Design Co** — both one-time, client-owns, with a self-edit CMS. Several "named" players turned out NOT to be peers (Hibu, Simple Website Service, Designjoy, Conversion Factory).

## TRUE one-time-fee / client-owns peers

### Agency7 — Edmonton · CLOSEST MATCH ⭐ · `high`
- **Model:** one-time project fee, 50% to book / 50% at launch.
- **Pricing (CAD, excl GST):** Starter Landing **$497** · Small Business **$997** · Business+Blog **$1,497** · E-commerce Starter **$1,997** · Custom Web App **$4,997+**.
- **Ownership:** verbatim *"You own everything — domain, code, content, hosting… We hand everything over at launch"* + *"No agency lock-in."*
- **CMS:** self-edit via integrated CMS (Sanity/MDX), with training + docs.
- **Optional add-ons:** non-binding Care plans ($99/mo Basic, $297/mo Growth, *"cancel any time… the site stays yours"*); copy help +$200, full copy+images +$600.
- **Why it matters:** the most complete structural twin of our model — ownership + one-time + self-edit + optional care. Their published 5-tier ladder and "no lock-in" language is a direct playbook to study. Note their **$497 landing entry undercuts our $1,800 Starter heavily.**
- Source: https://www.agency7.ca/affordable-web-design-edmonton

### Edmonton Web Design Co · `high`
- **Model:** one-time, 50% deposit / 50% at launch. *"0 Monthly Fees,"* *"Lifetime ownership,"* *"100% Ownership,"* *"no recurring fees, ever."*
- **Pricing (CAD):** Starter **$1,600** · Professional **$1,800–$5,000** · E-Commerce **$4,500–$7,000**. Only recurring option is optional SEO ($1,500+/mo).
- **Positioning:** explicitly against agencies charging $100–$300/mo. Multi-page custom, higher-priced than us.
- Source: https://edmontonwebdesignco.ca/

### Local Web Creator — Kitchener, ON (serves all Canada) · CHEAPEST PEER · `high`
- **Model:** one-time flat rate. Starter Website **$249 CAD one-time**.
- **Scope:** custom one-page site (up to 6 sections), mobile-responsive, contact form, Google Maps embed, basic SEO. 5–7 business-day turnaround, 2 revisions, 30-day support.
- **Ownership:** domain/hosting NOT included (client procures/owns separately ~$20–50/yr; they only guide). *Owns-everything is partly inferred — site doesn't explicitly say client owns source code.*
- **Gap vs us:** no advertised self-edit CMS, no LocalBusiness JSON-LD → **our Storyblok + schema is a real differentiator over the budget tier.** But $249 is an aggressive price anchor and they serve Calgary.
- Source: https://localwebcreator.ca/

### Sitebolt (sitebolt.co) — US · `high`
- **Model:** one-time flat fee, 50/50. Standard **$1,500** / Pro **$2,500**. *"No retainer, no lock-in, no monthly fees. You own it forever."*
- **GTM/process:** *"Websites for service businesses — built in 14 days,"* fully done-for-you via a single intake doc, **no calls/meetings.** Local-trade examples (e.g. epoxy flooring contractors).
- **Ownership:** full transfer at handoff; hosting is the lone client-managed cost.
- ⚠️ **Disambiguation:** sitebolt**.dev** is an unrelated $99/mo subscription — do not conflate. The peer is sitebolt**.co**.
- **Why it matters:** their "no calls, one intake doc, 14 days" async delivery is a sharp productization angle worth studying.
- Source: https://sitebolt.co/

### weCreate — US · `high`
- **Model:** one-time project fee. Starter Website **from $2,500**, 2–3 week turnaround. Four customizable templates, design/content integration, training, pre-launch testing. Hosting+maintenance is a separate optional add-on.
- **Fit:** one-time peer but aimed more at startups than hyper-local shops; higher entry.
- Source: https://www.wecreate.com/websites/starter-websites

### Mind2Market Group LLC — Florida · `medium` (2-1 split)
- Markets *"subscription-free / build it once and own it forever"* — client owns files/content/domain, only ongoing cost is annual domain renewal (<$15/yr).
- ⚠️ **Weakly supported:** it's content-marketing/advocacy, **not a pricing page** — their actual one-time fee is undisclosed, and they separately sell maintenance. No third-party (BBB/Trustpilot) corroboration. Treat as positioning, not a verified pricing peer.
- Source: https://mind2marketgroup.com/subscription-free-website-for-small-business/

## NOT peers (named last round — resolved this round)

### Hibu — locked-in subscription (the anti-us) · `high`
- One-time build **$99–$449** PLUS **$99–$159/mo** (hosting, security, support, maintenance) on a **proprietary closed platform**, **12-month no-cancel** contract.
- *"You do not own the design or the code; you are essentially renting"* — **cannot migrate** the site. Cancellation widely reported as very hard (BBB/Trustpilot/ComplaintsBoard).
- Domain (2-1): contractually client = registrant via Tucows reseller arrangement, but critics flag reseller-registrant risk.
- **Use:** the perfect *contrast/selling point* — everything our ownership pitch is against.
- Sources: https://www.websitebuilderexpert.com/designing-websites/hibu-review/ · https://hibu.com/legal/master-domain-registration-agreement · https://top10.com/website-builders/reviews/hibu

### Simple Website Service (Canada) — hybrid · `high`
- **$999 one-time** build PLUS **$99/mo "Peace of Mind"** (AWS hosting, backup, security, ongoing Google-guideline updates). Month-to-month, no contract, ~15 biz-day turnaround.
- Because the monthly **bundles hosting, the provider retains hosting** — a hybrid, not pure one-time/client-owns.
- Source: https://simplewebsiteservice.ca/

### Designjoy — productized-design subscription (model reference only) · `high`
- **$4,995/mo** (off regular $5,995; Pro ~$7,995/mo) for **unlimited design requests, one at a time**, done-for-you via Trello queue, avg 48-hr delivery, pause/cancel anytime.
- Famous "productized service" archetype but targets ongoing design for agencies/startups — **model + market mismatch** with us. Useful only as a productized-pricing reference.
- Source: https://www.designjoy.co/

### Conversion Factory — SaaS marketing retainer (out of niche) · `high`
- On-demand marketing/design/dev **subscription** for **SaaS/startups**, no contracts, entry via a **$1,000 paid Marketing Audit**. Explicitly does *not* serve retail/hospitality/local service businesses. (Has a secondary fixed-price website tier.)
- **Use:** their paid-$1,000-audit funnel is an interesting contrast to our *free* audit funnel.
- Sources: https://conversionfactory.co/ · https://conversionfactory.co/about

## Notes / open threads for later rounds
- **No verified Calgary-local one-time-fee rival yet** — surfaced Edmonton, Kitchener, US. Calgary home-market density still unquantified.
- **Self-edit CMS may be a defensible differentiator** — only Agency7 + Edmonton Web Design Co clearly ship ownership + self-edit CMS; cheaper peers (Local Web Creator, Sitebolt) appear to lack a self-edit CMS, and none advertise LocalBusiness JSON-LD like us.
- **GTM funnels of the one-time peers (Agency7, EWDC, Local Web Creator, Sitebolt, weCreate) not yet established** — audit funnel vs cold outreach vs templates/SEO. Worth a dedicated round.
- **Price-anchor pressure intensifies:** Agency7 $497 and Local Web Creator $249 both sit far below our $1,800 entry while claiming ownership — strengthens the case for either a low-anchor express tier or a hard TCO pitch.
