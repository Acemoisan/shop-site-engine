One-Time Finishing Steps

1 · Point your domain (astrosystems.ca) at the new site

Your web address stays exactly the same — we just aim it at the new, secure site. Zero downtime: the old site stays live until you approve this link, then we cut over. This also gives astrosystems.ca HTTPS for the first time, ending the "Not Secure" warning. The move is two small DNS records at your registrar — we do it for you in about 5 minutes once you tell us where the domain is registered and where your email is hosted (so we preserve your @astrosystems.ca email untouched).

2 · Send quote requests to your own inbox

The form is live now. Generate a free Web3Forms key at web3forms.com using info@astrosystems.ca, send it to us, and we’ll swap it in so every quote request lands directly in your inbox instead of ours.

3 · Optional — add real photos & self-serve editing

Two easy add-ons whenever you’re ready: real project photography placed throughout the site, and a simple CMS so you can edit and publish content yourself. Neither is needed for launch.
