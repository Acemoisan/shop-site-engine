Built Fast & Simple — On Purpose

Your site is a static Astro build: the pages are pre-made files served straight from a fast global host, with no database or heavy page-builder loading behind every visit. That is what keeps it quick on phones and dependable.

Plain version: instead of the site assembling itself every time someone visits (slow), the whole site is built once, ahead of time, and handed out instantly.

The Contact Form

It works right now

When a guest sends an enquiry, the message is delivered by a form service (Web3Forms). Today those messages arrive in our Studio0rbit inbox so we can confirm everything is flowing during the demo.

On engagement, it becomes yours

When you come on board, we swap one setting — the form's access key — to your own free Web3Forms key, tied to the inbox you choose. From then on every enquiry lands directly in your email. No account of ours sits in the middle.

Editing Your Content

Today: we make content changes for you

This site does not yet include a self-edit system. Your text, prices, and photos live in the site's code, so wording or image changes are a quick edit we make and re-publish for you — usually same-day. Nothing here locks you in or carries a monthly fee.

Optional add-on: self-editing (Storyblok)

If you'd like to change copy, prices, and images yourself — no code, no waiting — we can wire the site to Storyblok, a simple visual editor where you click a block, edit it, and hit Publish. That's an add-on we can quote separately; it is not part of this build.
