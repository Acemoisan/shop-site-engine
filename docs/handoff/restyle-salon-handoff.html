Your Contact Form

How it works today

The booking form sends each enquiry instantly by email through a service called Web3Forms. Right now it's wired to a shared Studio0rbit demo key, so submissions land in our operator inbox while you review the demo.

When you engage us

We swap one line — the access_key — to your own free Web3Forms key (grab it in two minutes at web3forms.com with your salon email). From then on every enquiry goes straight to your inbox. No monthly fee, nothing to log into.

Editing Your Content

Today — code edits

This site was built without a CMS, by scope. Text, services and images live in the site's code, so changes are a quick edit-and-redeploy on our side.

Available add-on — self-editing CMS

If you'd like to edit text, prices and photos yourself — no developer — we can add a Storyblok CMS: log in, click a block, type, hit Publish, and the site updates on its own. It's an optional add-on, priced separately.
