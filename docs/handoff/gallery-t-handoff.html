Editing Your Site Today

No CMS yet — edits are code right now

This site ships as a fast static build with the content written in. Text, prices, services and photos are edited in the code and re-published by us. For most salons that’s fine — the content rarely changes. If you’d like to edit it yourself (change a service, swap a photo, update hours) without calling us, we offer a Storyblok CMS add-on: a simple visual editor where you click a block, change it, and hit Publish. That’s a paid extra — say the word and we wire it in.

About The Photos

Imagery is thin — by necessity, for now

Only three salon photos were available, so we leaned on type, whitespace and the brass accent to keep the page feeling rich rather than sparse — the gallery is intentionally curated, not padded with stock. If you’d like a fuller hero and a wider “from the chair” gallery, our photo-sourcing add-on brings in more variety (a short shoot or sourced imagery). Also a paid extra — and it’s the single biggest upgrade available to this page.
