Editing Your Site

  1. Sign in to app.storyblok.com with your email (after the invite above).
  2. Open the Bitcoin Manor space → the bitcoin-manor story.
  3. Edit any text, price, link, image, or the product cards — sections can be added, reordered, or removed.
  4. Click Publish (not just Save). See the next box about edits going live.
  5. Use History to restore any earlier version.

Two One-Time Finishing Steps

1 · Make Storyblok edits go live automatically

Today the site is a published build, so an edit shows up after a quick redeploy (Netlify → Deploys → “Trigger deploy”). To make Publish auto-update the site in ~1 minute, we connect the site’s repo + a build hook + a Storyblok webhook — ~10 minutes, then it’s hands-off.

2 · Get emailed when someone uses the form

Messages are already saved in Netlify → Forms. To also be emailed, go to Forms → Settings & notifications → Add notification → Email, and enter the inbox you want (e.g. support@bitcoinmanor.com). We left the destination for you to choose.

Going Live On Your Domain

The site is on a free *.netlify.app address so you can review it. When you’re ready, the cleanest option:

  • Point bitcoinmanor.com (or www) at the new site, and move the WooCommerce store to shop.bitcoinmanor.com that the “Shop” buttons link to.

In Netlify: Domain management → Add a domain → follow the DNS steps. HTTPS is automatic.
