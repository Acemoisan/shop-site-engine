## Part 3 — Terms (plain-language, lawyer to finalize)

**Scope.** This project covers the deliverables listed in the proposal. Anything not listed (extra pages, new features, design/layout changes) is out of scope and quoted separately.

**Payment.** 50% deposit to begin; 50% on launch/handover. Prices in CAD, plus 5% GST.

**Revisions.** Includes [2] rounds of revisions during the build. Further changes billed at [$X/hr or per-change].

**Content.** Client provides text, images, logo, hours, and account access promptly. Timelines start when deposit + content are received. We can create copy/source images as a paid add-on.

**Ownership & handover.** On final payment, the client owns the domain, hosting account, content/editor account, and the site. We transfer or set up all accounts in the client's name and retain no control.

**No maintenance.** This is a one-time build. We provide **no ongoing hosting management, monitoring, updates, or content changes** after handover. The site is low-maintenance by design. Future work is a new, separately-quoted engagement.

**Self-edit boundary.** The client can self-edit content (text, images, hours, contacts, links, menus). Structural, layout, or design changes are not self-serve and require a new paid engagement.

**Third-party services.** Bookings, payments, and e-commerce run on the client's own third-party accounts (e.g. Square, Stripe, Shopify); their fees and terms apply. We are not responsible for third-party outages, pricing, or policy changes.

**Privacy.** If the site collects personal information (contact forms, bookings), the client is the data controller and is responsible for compliance with **Alberta's PIPA** (the applicable Alberta private-sector privacy law — not PIPEDA, for an in-province local business). We include a basic privacy notice; legal review is the client's responsibility.

**Liability.** Work delivered "as is" after acceptance; our liability is limited to the fees paid. We're not liable for lost business, third-party failures, or post-handover changes made by the client.

**Cancellation.** If cancelled after work begins, the deposit covers work completed to date and is non-refundable.
