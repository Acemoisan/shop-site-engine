## FAQ
- **Do I really own it?** Yes — domain, hosting, editor, and code are all in your name. We hand over every login.
- **Are there monthly fees?** None to us. You pay only your domain renewal (~$15/yr) and any tools you already use (like Square).
- **Can I edit it myself?** Yes — text, photos, hours, menus, links, all through a simple visual editor. We train you at handoff.
- **What if I need a design change later?** Layout/design changes are a small one-time job — that's how we keep your price low and your monthly cost at zero.
- **How long does it take?** Usually days. We move fast.
- **What about bookings and payments?** Those run on your own Square/Stripe/Shopify account — your money, your fees. We set up the connection.
- **Do you do maintenance?** No ongoing contracts. Your site is low-maintenance by design (no plugins to update). Need a change down the road? We quote it as a one-off.
