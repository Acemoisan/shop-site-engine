## What the flat fee includes (the baseline promise)

- A fast, mobile-first site on our Astro engine (no WordPress treadmill).
- **Client owns all accounts** — domain registrar, hosting (free tier), Storyblok (free Starter), and the repo. One-time setup, then it's theirs.
- **Self-editable content** via Storyblok's visual editor: text, images, hours, contacts, links, menus.
- `LocalBusiness` structured data + Google Business Profile guidance so they're findable.
- Privacy note referencing **Alberta PIPA** (not PIPEDA) on any site collecting form/booking data.
- A handoff session + written guide.

## What's explicitly OUT of scope (protects the no-maintenance model)

- **No ongoing maintenance, hosting management, or content updates by us** after handoff.
- **Self-edit = content, not design.** Layout, navigation, new sections, or structural/visual redesign are **not** self-serve — they're a new paid mini-engagement (ties to the [scoping rubric](../research/2026-06-15-engagement-scoping-rubric.md): content = free self-edit; structure = re-quote).
- Bookings/payments run on the **client's own** business tools (Square/Stripe/Shopify) — their account, their fees. We wire the embed; we don't operate it.
- Logo design, professional photography, and ongoing SEO/ads are add-ons, not included.

## Engagement types → which package

Driven by the [scoping rubric](../research/2026-06-15-engagement-scoping-rubric.md) audit grade:

- **No site / only a social page** → the $1,500 flat-fee build (+ any add-ons by need).
- **Audit grade D–F (systemic failure)** → the $1,500 flat-fee rebuild + migration add-on.
- **Audit grade B–C (solid base, surface issues)** → **targeted "tune-up"** mini-engagement, not a full package.
- **Audit grade A (already good)** → be honest, decline or offer a small specific fix. Builds trust/referrals.

## Margin logic (why this works at one-time pricing)

- Our cost floor is ~$0–15 (Astro/Tailwind/shadcn/Storyblok free; our domain ~$15/yr).
- The AI pipeline + design system make each build a token-swap + content fill, not a from-scratch project.
- Client recurring cost ≈ **~$15 CAD/yr** (domain only) — a genuine differentiator vs no-code's $10–39/mo.
- No recurring revenue, so **volume + turnaround speed** is the engine — which is exactly what the pipeline delivers.
