The Studio

I work in the dark on purpose.

NORTHLIGHT is a one-photographer studio in Inglewood. I shoot weddings, editorial, and brand work the same way: slowly, with available light, looking for the moment just before or just after the obvious one.

My frames lean film, high-contrast, and unhurried — pictures that remember how it felt rather than how it looked. Based in Calgary, available across Alberta and beyond.
