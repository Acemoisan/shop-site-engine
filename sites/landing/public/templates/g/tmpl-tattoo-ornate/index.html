Before You Sit

Policies & Aftercare

Do I need ID?+

Yes — you must be 18+ with valid government photo ID. No exceptions, every visit.

How do deposits work?+

An $80 non-refundable deposit holds your slot and comes off your final price. 48-hour reschedule notice required.

What about touch-ups?+

We provide an aftercare sheet and offer complimentary touch-ups on our own work within 90 days.

Walk-ins or appointment?+

Both. Custom work is by appointment; flash and small pieces welcome walk-ins when an artist is free.
