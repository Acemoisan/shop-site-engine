Our Story

A boutique brokerage, built on trust.

Meridian & Vale was founded on a simple conviction: that the most significant transaction of a person's life deserves more than a sign on a lawn. We work with a deliberately small roster of clients, allowing every listing the attention, marketing, and discretion it warrants.

Founder Eleanor Vale has spent nearly two decades representing Calgary's most distinctive homes — heritage estates in Mount Royal, riverside properties in Roxboro, and architectural statements across Britannia and Eagle Ridge. The result is a practice defined by restraint, craft, and results.
