Our Story

Grain, Water, Hops & The Block We Love

Ironwood started with a homebrew kit, a corner unit on Brewery Lane, and a stubborn belief that the freshest beer is the one made closest to the glass it's poured into. Ten years later, we're still two blocks from where you're standing.

We brew small so we can brew often — chasing the harvest, the seasonal hops, and the styles our regulars keep asking for. No macro shortcuts, no shipping it across the country to go stale. Just honest beer for the neighborhood that raised us.

  • Independent & family-run — no investors telling us what to pour.
  • Locally sourced — Alberta grain, Pacific Northwest hops, Bow River water.
  • Always fresh — most kegs hit the tap within two weeks of brew day.
