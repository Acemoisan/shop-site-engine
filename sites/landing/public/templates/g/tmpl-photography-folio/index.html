The Studio

I make pictures of people
the way film remembers them.

Northlight is a one-woman studio in Inglewood. After a decade shooting weddings and editorial across the prairies, I learned the same thing every season: the best frames are the unhurried ones. So I work quietly, on warm film-leaning tones, and I never rush a room.

Half-day or full, brand or wedding — you get a calm presence behind the lens and a gallery that still feels honest a decade from now.
