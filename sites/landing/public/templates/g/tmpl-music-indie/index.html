The band

Four friends, one tape machine.

Velvet Static started as late-night demos passed around on a single cassette. Five years and a hundred shows later, they're still chasing the same feeling — the warmth right after the lights go down.

'Afterglow' is their most intimate record yet: analog, unhurried, and recorded almost entirely live to tape at Hightide Sound.

"The late-night record Calgary didn't know it needed."— CJSW 90.9
