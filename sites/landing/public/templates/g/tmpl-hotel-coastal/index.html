The Welcome

Stay a while.
The world can wait.

Tucked between the dunes and the cedar line, Marisol Coastal is twenty-two rooms of quiet light and open horizon. We built it for slow mornings — bare feet on warm wood, coffee carried to the balcony, the long exhale that only the sea seems to allow.

No crowds, no rush. Just salt air, soft linen, and a team who learns your name by the first morning.

Dining

From the day boat
to your table.
