The Experience

Where provenance meets the plate.

Every evening at Maison Lumiere begins with a single ingredient and ends with a memory. Our kitchen draws on the rhythm of the Alberta seasons — foraged herbs, dry-aged beef, the first beets of autumn — plated with French intention and quiet confidence.

There is no rush here. Eight courses unfold across a single, candlelit evening, paced by a sommelier who knows when a pour should whisper and when it should sing.

— Chef Élodie Marchand
