Before you book

Good to know

What do I need to book?

You must be 18+ with valid government photo ID. No exceptions. An $80 non-refundable deposit holds your slot and comes off your final price.

How does the deposit work?

Deposits secure your appointment and are applied to your session total. We require 48-hour notice to reschedule without forfeiting it.

Do you offer touch-ups?

Yes — free touch-ups on our work within 90 days. An aftercare sheet is provided with every piece.

Walk-ins or appointment?

Both. Custom work is by appointment; flash and small pieces are often available as walk-ins Tuesday through Saturday.
