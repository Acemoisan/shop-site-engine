The Experience

Where provenance meets the plate.

Each evening unfolds as a single, considered sequence — eight courses paced to the room, not the clock. We cook with what Alberta's growers, foragers and ranchers send us that week, in the precise French idiom.

Nothing is rushed. The wine arrives a beat before the dish it was chosen for. The lighting softens as the night deepens. You leave having been somewhere, not simply having eaten.

  • Sourced within the province, plated in the French canon
  • A sommelier-led pairing for every course
  • Last seating ninety minutes before close
