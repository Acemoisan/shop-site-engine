About

A photographer who works quietly and sees clearly.

I’m Elise. For nearly a decade I’ve made pictures across Alberta and beyond — weddings that feel like the day actually felt, brand work that says something, and portraits people are relieved to look at.

My approach is simple: stay out of the way, chase good light, and trust the moment. The result is unhurried and a little film-leaning — images built to outlast the trend cycle.
