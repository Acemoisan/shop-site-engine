Good to know

Questions, answered

Still curious? We'll cover everything at your free consultation.

Is there downtime?

Most treatments have little to none — we'll review exactly what to expect at your free consultation.

Who performs the treatments?

All injectables are performed by our licensed medical team under physician oversight.

How do I prepare for my visit?

Arrive with a clean face and avoid blood-thinning supplements for 48 hours before injectables. We'll send full guidance at booking.

Will results look natural?

That's our entire philosophy. We tailor every plan to refine, never to alter — you, only more rested.
