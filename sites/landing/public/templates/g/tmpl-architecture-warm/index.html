Featured · Heritage Mill Reuse

An awkward heritage shell, reworked into the city's best room.

A century-old mill in Ramsay, returned to use as a restaurant. We preserved the masonry and timber bones, then layered warm plaster, brass, and tobacco leather to let golden-hour light do the rest.
