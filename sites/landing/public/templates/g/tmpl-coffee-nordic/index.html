Our Craft

From the farm to your favourite cup.

We roast in small batches every week, a few kilos at a time, so the bag you take home tastes the way it did the morning it left the drum. Nothing sits. Nothing dulls.

Our greens come from growers we know by name — washed lots from the highlands of Guji, honey-processed cherries from Huila. We chase clarity over char, and we keep it unpretentious but precise.

  1. 01 Source single lots, traceable to the farm.
  2. 02 Roast weekly, in small drum batches.
  3. 03 Brew slow, taste, and start again.
