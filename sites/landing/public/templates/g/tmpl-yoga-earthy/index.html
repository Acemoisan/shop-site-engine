The studio

A practice for every body, every day.

Lumen & Lotus is a light-filled studio in Calgary's Inglewood district where modern movement meets ancient practice. Whether it's your first sun salutation or your thousandth, you'll find a warm, unhurried space to reconnect with your body and quiet the noise.

  • Real teachers, real light, no ego.
  • Heated wood floors and linen-soft props.
  • Tea and stillness after every class.
