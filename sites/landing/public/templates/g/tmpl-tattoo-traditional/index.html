Before You Sit

House Rules & FAQ

How old do I need to be?

Must be 18+ with valid government photo ID. No exceptions, no exceptions for parents either.

How do deposits work?

An $80 non-refundable deposit holds your slot and comes off your final price. 48-hour reschedule notice required.

Do you do touch-ups?

Free touch-ups on our work within 90 days. We provide a full aftercare sheet at the end of every session.

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes — grab a flash off the board any open day. Custom pieces are by appointment so we can draw it right.
