Our Craft

Every piece is hand-finished in our Calgary atelier. No two are exactly alike.

We start with reclaimed gold, melt and pour it ourselves, and spend the hours a machine never would — filing, setting, and polishing until the metal feels alive. The small irregularities aren't flaws. They're the signature of a human hand.

01

03

Finish & sign

Polished, hallmarked, and made to be passed on.
