Our Story

Quiet brilliance, made by hand.

Aurelle & Vale began at a single bench on 17th Avenue — one jeweller, one loupe, and a belief that fine jewelry should be worn every day rather than locked away for someday.

We work in recycled 14k and 18k gold and set only certified stones. Each piece is finished entirely by hand, which is why the gold catches light a little differently from one ring to the next. That is the point.
