The Brewhouse
Our Story

Refined Grit, Brewed In Inglewood

We built Ironwood in an old machine shop in 2014 — kept the corrugated walls, the concrete, the bones. The copper kettles went in where the lathes came out. Everything you drink here is brewed in the room you're standing in.

Independent and family-run. We source Alberta barley and chase the freshest hops we can get our hands on. No shortcuts, no contract brewing, no corporate parent. Just honest beer for the neighborhood that built us.

  • 100% independent
  • Local Alberta grain
  • Zero contract brewing
