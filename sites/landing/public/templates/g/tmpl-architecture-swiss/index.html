A.02 — Studio

We design slowly and build to last — favouring honest materials, generous daylight, and proportion over ornament.

Meridian & Co. is a Calgary architecture and interiors studio working across private residences, hospitality, and adaptive reuse. Every project begins with how a space should feel before it considers how it should look.

A.05 — Case Study

Mount Royal Residence

A heritage lot reworked around a single north-light void. The brief asked for calm; the answer was a board-formed concrete spine, white oak floors, and rooms that borrow daylight from one another.
